Welcome to my digital garden. I navigate the worlds of wealth management, healthcare, and technology, but at my core, I am simply a lifelong student. This space is where I document my professional experiments, personal reflections, and the quiet endurance required to grow in both.

About Me

My journey didn't start in finance; it began in Biomedical Engineering at Tianjin Medical University. Transitioning from medical device sales to wealth management was a leap of faith that taught me resilience. While I'm proud of milestones like becoming a top-performing wealth manager at CITIC Securities, I value the lessons learned from the pressure and the deep relationships built with clients far more than the accolades.

I approach life much like I approach a marathon: with patience, pacing, and a respect for the long game. Whether I'm analyzing market trends, running my charitable micro-fund ("On the way365"), or simply reading a good book, I try to stay grounded. I believe that true expertise isn't just about data-driven results; it's about empathy, curiosity, and knowing how to endure through complex challenges.

Lately, I find myself fascinated by the messy, exciting intersection of AI, finance, and healthcare. I spend my days tinkering with quantitative trading algorithms, exploring biomedical signal processing (like BCG), and building tools to aggregate global news. It's a humbling process of constant trial and error, balancing ambitious projects like CFA preparation with the simple joy of learning how to build things that matter.

My Journey

2023 - 2025

Exploring AI & Quant Systems

Dove deep into automation and data. Built live quantitative trading algorithms, deployed an AI WeChat consultant, and developed a global news aggregator to curate insights. A period defined by intense self-teaching and experimentation.

2020 - 2023

Embracing Financial Markets

Transitioned into finance, starting as a Product Manager at China Construction Bank before moving to CITIC Securities. By leveraging deep market research and client relationship skills, I became the top-performing wealth manager in my branch.

2019 - 2020

Medical Device Sales Representative

Pioneered regional medical equipment sales for Hangzhou Langjun Medical, conducting extensive market research and successfully managing bidding processes for hospitals.

2016 - Present

Co-Founder of "On the way365" Micro-Fund

Launched a grassroots charity fund where members donate 1 RMB daily. Over the past 7 years, we have raised over 35,000 RMB to financially support middle school students in need.

2015 - 2019

B.S. in Biomedical Engineering

Graduated from Tianjin Medical University. Built a strong foundation in medical devices, served as Minister of the Work-Study Center, and founded a campus tutoring platform providing over 1,000 part-time opportunities for students.
